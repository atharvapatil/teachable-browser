Sit straight

An teachable machine experiment which forces you to sit straight while watching videos.

If you tilt your head or don't sit straight the video blurs out making it impossible for you to watch the video unless you sit straight.

Adjust your position to the face marker & begin the experiment

How it works ...

  • Using webcam & machine learning, keypoints [1] relevant to the face & shoulder are identified.
  • The algorithm is trained to identify good [2] & bad [3] poses, independent of users.
  • When bad poses are identified the screen blurs out. Try adjusting your position to the face marker [4] & click to continue.
